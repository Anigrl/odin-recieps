RASGULLA

Description

rasgulla is a sweet dish which is found mainly in india pakistan and bangladesh. it is very sweet in taste. it is made of rice flour and sugar.

INGREDIENTS

  • milk
  • corn starch
  • lemon juice
  • sugar
